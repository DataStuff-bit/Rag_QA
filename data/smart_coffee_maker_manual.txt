# Product Manual: Smart Coffee Maker X2000

## Overview
The Smart Coffee Maker X2000 is a connected beverage appliance featuring WiFi connectivity, mobile app control, and programmable brewing schedules.

## Specifications
- Capacity: 12 cups (1.8L)
- Brew Time: 5-8 minutes
- Temperature: Adjustable 185-205°F (85-96°C)
- Power: 1200W
- WiFi: 802.11b/g/n (2.4GHz)
- App: Compatible with iOS 12+ and Android 7+

## Quick Start
1. Fill water reservoir with fresh, cold water
2. Add ground coffee to filter basket (1 tablespoon per cup)
3. Press power button or use mobile app
4. Brew completes when all water is dispensed

## Mobile App Features
- Schedule brewing up to 7 days in advance
- Monitor brew progress in real-time
- Adjust temperature remotely
- Receive notifications when coffee is ready
- View brewing history and statistics

## Troubleshooting

### Coffee is too weak
- Use more coffee grounds (currently using 1 tablespoon per cup)
- Increase brew time in settings
- Ensure water temperature is set to maximum (205°F)

### WiFi connection fails
- Restart the device (unplug for 30 seconds)
- Ensure 2.4GHz WiFi network is available
- Check WiFi password is correct in app settings
- Factory reset if problem persists (hold reset button 10 seconds)

### App shows "Device Offline"
- Check your device is powered on
- Verify WiFi network is accessible
- Check router firewall isn't blocking the device
- Reinstall the mobile app and reconnect
